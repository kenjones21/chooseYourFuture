Budgets in this document are based primarily on the budgets laid out in Table 2.2 of the AR5 Synthesis report. This table was used to create the divisions in the emissions pathway chart as well as the temperature corresponding to a 33% chance of exceedance.

However, it was not possible to use this table to inform probabilities of exceedance for intermediate levels of emissions. To create this, the AR5 scenario database was used. In the database, probabilities of exceedance are given for 1.5, 2, 3, and 4 degrees Celsius across model runs. It is possible to compare cumulative emissions to 2100 and probabilities of exceedance.
